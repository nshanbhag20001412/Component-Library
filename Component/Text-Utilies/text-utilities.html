Miscellaneous

Some of the defaulted cases. You can overight them accordingly.

  • Paragraphs are by defaulted to be justified from both the sides.
  • Lists dont have bullets for all components. They have a margin of 1rem top and bottom.
  • Anchor tags have no text decoration and they inherit the color from parent.
