Badge

Here you can find different badge like on text, on button and on a icon. Just copy the code below and you are all set!

Lorem, ipsum.
4
4
//badge on text
<div class="badge">
<div class="text">
Lorem, ipsum.
<div class="badge-icon">4</div>
</div>
</div>

//badge on button
<div class="badge">
<button class="button-container">
Button
<div class="badge-icon">4</div>
</button>
</div>

//badge on icon
<div class="badge badge-icons">
<div class="button-container">
<i class="fas fa-home fa-2x"></i>
<div class="badge-icon">4</div>
</div>
</div>
