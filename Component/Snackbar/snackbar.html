Snackbar

Snackbar is something which is triggered and is displayed at the bottom of viewport. It informs the user about the process which is going to be performed or is going to be performed.

Baseline snackbar

Using this you can have you snackbar to bottom and aligned center of the viewport

Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
Retry
<div class="snackbar snackbar-baseline">
<div class="text">
Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
</div>
<a class=" link snackbar-link" href="">Retry</a>
<i class="fas fa-times"></i>
</div>

Leading

Leading snackbar can be used to display your snackbar at the bottom and align it to the start

Your photo has been archived.
Undo
<div class="snackbar snackbar-leading">
<div class="text">

</div>
