Avatar

Image Avatars

Below the second avatar is the default size. According to your requirments you can use the smaller or the larger version on it.

<img class="avatar" src="/Images/girl.png" alt=""> //Add class "avatar" to your <img> to use this

<img class="avatar sm" src="/Images/girl.png" alt=""> //Add "sm" to your <img> for size small

<img class="avatar lg" src="/Images/girl.png" alt=""> //Add "lg" to your <img> for size small

Other Variation

You can find the other variations of the avatar below. Just add the class and you good to go!

NS
<div class=" avatar avatar-text" >NS</div> //instead of using img add the avatar class to yr element

<div class="avatar avatar-icon" ><i class="fas fa-folder"></i></div> //Add "avatar-icon" to the div of the icon for icon avatar

<img class="avatar avatar-square" src="/Images/girl.png" alt=""> //Add "avatar-square" to your <img> for square avatar
